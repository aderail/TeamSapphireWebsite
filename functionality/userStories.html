User Stories

Heavy Sleeper: A user who has difficulty waking up easily and often sleeps through standard alarms.
Light Sleeper: A user who wakes up easily form small noises or notifications and values an undisturbed sleep.
Restless Sleeper: A user who struggles to stay asleep throughout the night or experiences frequent restlessness and poor-quality sleep.
Consistent Sleeper: A user who goes to bed and wakes up at roughly the same time every day, including weekends, maintaining a regular sleep schedule.
Student: A user balancing academic responsibilities.
Shift Worker: A user whose sleep schedule varies due to alternating or overnight work shifts.
Night Owl: A user who tends to stay up late, often past typical bedtime hours, due to preference or habit.
Creator: A content contributor who produces sleep-related media.
Health Conscious: A user who actively monitors their wellness, including sleep, diet, and fitness, and wants their sleep data to align with broader health goals.
