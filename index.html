Welcome to SleepSync

"Get in Sync Catch More Z's"

Elevator Pitch

SleepSync helps late night coders and gamers build healthier sleep habits by gently aligning screen time with personalized wind‑down routines. With smart prompts and progress insights, SleepSync makes it easier to log off, relax, and wake up refreshed.

The Problem

Staring at a computer screen all night can make falling asleep difficult. Individuals can easily lose track of time, news and media stimulants keep the brain active, and the blue light reduces melatonin production. Due to these factors, coders and gamers are generally known to have irregular sleep schedules.

When people stay up late, they need to wake up later to retain enough sleep. However, not everyone’s schedule allows time for late mornings despite staying up late. They still need to be up for work, class, fitness, etc. This leads to coders and gamers getting a lot less sleep than they need.

Our Customers and Users

The primary users of the sleep app include parents, students, and general tech users seeking to improve their sleep habits and overall well-being. Parents may use the app to monitor their sleep patterns, establish bedtime routines for their children, or access guided relaxation tools to help the whole family rest better. Students, often facing irregular schedules and high stress, can use features like sleep tracking, breathing exercises, and relaxing music to promote healthier sleep cycles and improve academic performance. Tech users may also use the app to manage screen time before bed, track sleep data, and utilize the automatic blue light for easier rest.

The customers for the app extend beyond individual users to include professionals and organizations that might recommend it as a supportive tool. These include psychologists, therapists, pediatricians, and wellness coaches who can refer the app to their clients as part of treatment or lifestyle guidance. Schools, universities, and workplaces may also serve as customers by promoting or integrating the app into wellness programs to help students and employees manage stress and sleep health.

By catering to both direct users and professional customers, the app positions itself as a versatile resource that bridges personal self-care and expert-guided health support. This dual focus helps expand the app’s impact — improving individual sleep outcomes while empowering professionals to extend their care beyond traditional sessions or office visits.

Our Solution

SleepSync is an application designed to help users maintain a healthy sleep schedule by calculating the ideal bedtime based off of users ideal wake-up time. The app sends personalized reminders during designated “wind down” periods that encourage users to begin preparing for rest.

SleepSync also offers relaxing music, tips for falling asleep faster, and more to create a calming nighttime routine. Together, these features make SleepSync more than just an alarm — it’s a sleep companion.
